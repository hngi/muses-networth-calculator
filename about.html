Muses NetWorth Calculator

The Muses NetWorth Calculator is a full scale web application that gives users real time information about their assets and liabilities in turn, helping them keep track of their Net Worth. This is not just an average calculator that gives out the difference between assets and liabilities. Our NetWorth calculator enables users create a free account so that they can always login and have access to their data among other interesting features.

With our NetWorth calculator, you can add an unlimited number of assets and liabilities and can sort and filter these items based on date/time added, description or monetary value.

You can also edit and delete items added. In the process of all these operations, your data are being stored and updated simultaneously when necessary so when you login some other time, you can easily access all your information and perform other operations

A dedicated statistics page contains valuable information about the total values of your assets, liabilities and net worth. You can also get these values immediately after adding, updating or deleting an item.

Your total net worth also enables us accurately calculate the percentile you fall in globally and your present ranking in Nigeria's richest.
